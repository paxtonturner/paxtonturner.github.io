Paxton Turner


I am a postdoc in the Department of Statistics at Harvard University working with Zheng Tracy Ke. I obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of Mathematics at MIT, advised by Philippe Rigollet. My interests are broadly in statistics and machine learning. Recently I have worked on testing discrete distributions (such as count datasets and networks), data compression, and covariate balancing. Tools from high-dimensional statistics and discrete mathematics play an especially important role in my research. Here is my C.V.
